AI LAWS & REGULATIONS — SUMMARY (TXT)

Course: CSC 160 — AI for All
Date: Nov 11, 2025

TOPICS:
- Federal AI laws (current + historical)
- Executive Orders on AI (Biden + Trump)
- State-level AI regulations (NY, UT, CA, TN)
- Legal fault lines: privacy, copyright, minors, safety, transparency

FEDERAL LANDSCAPE:
- NAIIA (2020): National AI coordination, research goals, committees.
- Executive Order 14110 (Biden, 2023):
  - Safety-focused, model vetting, compute thresholds.
  - Required government evaluation of frontier models.
  - Rescinded by Trump in 2025.

- Trump Administration (2025):
  - “Preventing Woke AI” order
    • Requires LLMs used by government to be truthful, neutral, non-ideological.
  - “America’s AI Action Plan”
    • Focus on innovation, infrastructure, national security.

- Congress (118th/119th):
  - 150+ bills introduced, almost none passed.
  - Many proposals focus on transparency, national security, copyright.

- “TAKE IT DOWN” Act (2024):
  - Criminalizes non-consensual intimate imagery (NCII).
  - Requires platforms to remove NCII within 48 hours.

STATE REGULATIONS:
New York:
- LOADinG Act: oversight of automated decision systems in government.
- AI Companion Limitations: protections for minors using AI companions.
- RAISE Act: frontier model restrictions (awaiting signature).
- NY AI Act: large framework (still in committee).

California:
- Laws addressing:
  • Algorithmic pricing
  • Chatbot regulation
  • Frontier model transparency
  • Deepfake pornography
  • Law enforcement use of AI
  • Healthcare accuracy
  • Data center energy use

Utah:
- AI disclosure laws (must notify users when interacting with AI)
- Identity protections
- Liability for harmful AI outputs

Tennessee:
- “ELVIS Act”: prohibits voice cloning of artists without consent.

KEY FAULT LINES:
- IP/Copyright of AI outputs
- Deepfakes / NCII
- Risks to minors from AI companions
- Privacy & data ownership
- Algorithmic bias (hiring, lending, policing)
- National security & compute restrictions
- Government use of AI
- Safety + rogue AI concerns
